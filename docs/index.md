# Home

[Hyli](https://hyli.org/) is a confidential yet verifiable financial settlement infrastructure for European regulated actors.

It enables regulated institutions to settle, tokenize, and comply without ever exposing sensitive data.

## What you can build

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-bank-outline:{ .lg .middle } __Private stablecoin issuance__

    ---

    Deploy MiCA-compliant EUR stablecoins with transaction-level privacy and selective disclosure for regulators.

-   :material-file-document-outline:{ .lg .middle } __Confidential asset tokenization__

    ---

    Tokenize real-world assets with private investor positions and regulatory transparency on request.

-   :material-swap-horizontal:{ .lg .middle } __Institutional payment networks__

    ---

    Build B2B payment infrastructure where amounts, counterparties, and timing remain confidential.

</div>

## What you get with Hyli

- __Privacy by default__: transaction details never touch the public ledger.
- __Selective disclosure__: regulators and auditors access what they need, on request.
- __Cryptographic settlement guarantees__: outcomes are proven, not trusted.
- __Designed for European regulation__: built for entities subject to MiCA and AML/CTF requirements.
